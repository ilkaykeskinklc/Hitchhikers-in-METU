Hitchhikers


Hitchhiker was designed by CEIT students in 2021. Hitchhiker is entirely free and does not charge any fees. The purpose of this application is to provide accessible transportation, especially to METU students.









Mission

Hitchhiker is a ride-sharing platform that brings together drivers traveling with empty seats in their vehicles and passengers who want to go in the same direction. Hitchhikers, which provides transportation to the Middle East Technical University and some points of Ankara, provides convenience to people with transportation problems. Hitchhiker provides opportunities not only to students at Middle East Technical University but also to any member of our school.

Vision

Hitchhiker aims to enable METU students to reach the places they can go quickly. "Hitchhiker," which provides free travel, will be a life-saving project for students.
