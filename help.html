Frequently Asked Questions



What is the purpose?

Hitchhiker is a car-sharing service that connects drivers with empty seats in their vehicles with passengers traveling in the same direction. Also, hitchhikers, who give transportation to Middle ast Technical University and other parts of Ankara, make life easier for those who are not own a car. Hitchhikers offer transportation opportunities to Middle East Technical University students and every member of our school.

How to use

People have the chance to choose a vehicle according to the place and time they want to go.

Who can benefit?

In order to benefit from this application, you must first be a student or a member of a Middle East Technical University.

How to register

After clicking on the profile section in the application, the necessary information must be filled in. (The information must be entered correctly.)

Is there a cost?

The application is entirely free. It does not charge any fees.

Is it safe?

It is absolutely dependable. The privacy of all users is fully protected.
